Robotics

VEX Asia Pacific Open with CREATE

A global hands-on robotics challenge where students design, build, and compete.

VEX is one of the world’s largest student robotics competitions, with over 1 million students across 70+ countries, and participation in South Korea continues to grow rapidly each year.

By launching the VEX Asia Pacific Open in partnership with CREATE, we are providing students with an additional high-level competition opportunity beyond the national rounds hosted by REC, opening a new pathway for teams to compete, collaborate, and challenge themselves on an international stage.

  • One of the world’s largest student robotics competitions.
  • VEX Asia Pacific Open currently features IQ and V5.
  • Standardized rules ensure equal competition with international teams.
NEW
The VEX Asia Pacific Open will launch for the first time in South Korea in collaboration with the CREATE organization, welcoming both local and international teams. The event is confirmed for January for the VEX IQ division, with details for the VEX V5 division to be announced soon.

Our Collaborating Partners

We cooperate with esteemed educational organizations, independent organizers, and global leaders in STEM and academic competitions to deliver world-class programs.

CREATE Foundation (USA)

The CREATE Foundation is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to supporting educational organizations, students, teachers, and mentors in advancing STEM education. The Foundation supports robotics competitions that provide a unique, fun, and challenging year-round experience, strengthening technical knowledge, teamwork, communication, and leadership skills. CREATE hosts workshops, seminars, scrimmages, and tournaments, including their signature event, the CREATE U.S. Open Robotics Championship.

LUMINAS

LUMINAS is a non-profit organization with an initiative dedicated to unlocking the potential of young minds globally. Their mission is to create transformative opportunities for students to showcase their individual skill sets and personality on international stages through contests and educational programs.
